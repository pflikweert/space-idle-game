## Technical Info
- **Format:** PNG with alpha transparency
- **Background:** Transparent
- **Resolution:** 1024×1024 total sheet
- **Cell size:** ~205×512 per effect (5 cols × 2 rows)
- **Integration:** Crop each cell, import as individual sprite in Godot
- **Blending:** Use additive blending in Godot for glow effects (CanvasItemMaterial → Blend Mode: Add)
